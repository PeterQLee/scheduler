Welcome to the University Scheduler, scroll down to get help on various topics.

Registering and Logging in.

In order to use this website, you will need to make an account. Please register by entering your email and password in the "Sign-Up" box.
Once you have done that, proceed to the box labelled "Login Required" to use the site.

Selecting/Adding Courses

To select your courses click on the checkbox adjacent to your desired course and click on the "Choose Courses" button. 
If you do not see your desired course in the list, feel free to add it yourself by typing in its name, time span, days of the week, and its description. Make sure your entry for the start-time and end-time are 4 digit and appropriate 2400h times. Once you have added a course, it will be visible to all users.

Make your schedule

This page will help you see how courses will fit into your timetable. Select courses by clicking on their checkboxes and view the dynamic timetable below to see which time blocks it occupies. Blue rectangles indicate that the timeslot is ok, where red rectangles indicate conflicting timeslots. 

Selection Options

This page shows the number of different options of how you can choose your courses assuming each course in your selection with a unique name is chosen once.
